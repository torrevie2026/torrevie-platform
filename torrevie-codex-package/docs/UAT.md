# Torrevie FSM UAT

Run these paths after a staging deployment with synthetic tenants. Use one tenant per segment.

## SOLO

1. Sign in as the owner.
2. Open Torrevie FSM.
3. Confirm the menu shows Today, Jobs, WhatsApp Inbox, Customers, Quotes and Invoices, ROI, Settings.
4. Open onboarding.
5. Select homeowner service, owner WhatsApp intake, and up to 5 field people.
6. Confirm segment `SOLO` and Entry plan.
7. Save onboarding.
8. Open Channel Hub and create a WhatsApp intake request.
9. Open ROI and confirm the request count increases.
10. Save ROI settings with 20 admin minutes per request.

## TRADE

1. Sign in as a dispatcher.
2. Open Torrevie FSM.
3. Confirm Growth features show PM Calendar, Contracts, Assets, WhatsApp Inbox, Reports.
4. Open onboarding.
5. Select businesses under maintenance contracts, shared inbox, and 6 to 50 field people.
6. Confirm segment `TRADE` and Growth plan.
7. Save onboarding.
8. Create a manual portal intake request.
9. Open Channel Hub and confirm unified triage shows the request.
10. Open ROI and confirm channel capture includes portal.

## FM

1. Sign in as an operations manager.
2. Open Torrevie FSM.
3. Confirm the dashboard title is Command Center.
4. Confirm Channel Hub and SLA Board are visible when entitled.
5. Open Channel Hub.
6. Request voice setup with forwarded existing number.
7. Confirm status is pending.
8. Confirm monthly voice cap is shown.
9. Open ROI and confirm after-hours capture is shown.
10. Confirm client report pack state follows Enterprise entitlement.

## COMMUNITY

1. Sign in as a community manager.
2. Open Torrevie FSM.
3. Confirm terminology uses Resident, Building, Common Area Asset, Resident Request.
4. Open onboarding.
5. Select buildings and residents, hotline, and more than 50 field people.
6. Confirm segment `COMMUNITY`.
7. Save onboarding.
8. Create a portal intake request.
9. Open ROI and confirm the report section renders in RTL and LTR.
10. Confirm board report pack is locked unless Enterprise report entitlement is present.

## OEM

1. Sign in as an after-sales manager.
2. Open Torrevie FSM.
3. Confirm terminology uses Work Orders and Installed Product.
4. Open onboarding.
5. Select products we manufacture or distribute, email and dealer channels, and 6 to 50 field people.
6. Confirm segment `OEM` and Enterprise suggested tier.
7. Save onboarding.
8. Open Channel Hub.
9. Create an email intake request with a serial number in the summary.
10. Open ROI and confirm monthly value email text includes the Torrevie FSM footer.
